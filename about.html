About us

Bolingo was founded by a diverse group of youth, originally from the Democratic Republic of Congo. Destiny brought them together in South Africa. One day at a typical meeting at a park in KwaZulu-Natal, the land of King Shaka, precisely in Durban where issues affecting the Democratic Republic of Congo were discussed. They decided to make a change instead of just talking about it. Hence Bolingo the youth movement was formed, where anyone striving for change and truly believes in loving their neighbours as themselves could join.

"The reason and idea behind the organization is more spiritual, rather than material. Our vision is to achieve unity, peace and stability in the Democratic Republic of Congo and the rest of the world. "

Bolingo was formed in June 2015 as a strategy and tool to achieve this vision. The name Bolingo was intuitively chosen, because love is the only tool to achieve unity, peace and stability.

In January 2016, the team in South Africa formed an administrative team in the Democratic Republic of Congo to facilitate and execute all the ideas and projects. A few weeks down the line, an exchange student from Germany was attracted by their thoughts, destiny made it possible for them to meet. He joined the movement and the team was complete and well established.

Our Vision

”We want to give the children of the Democratic Republic of Congo the possibility of a better life”. We would like to break the barriers of poverty through improving the learning conditions of the children. This approach will motivate the youth to learn, to think out of the box and will produce young entrepreneurs. We want to build a better place, where nobody will need to cry and suffer from malnutrition and starvation. “Bolingo” acts according to the following Chinese Proverb: “Give a man a fish and you feed him for a day, teach a man how to fish and you feed him for a lifetime.” We want to give the children and the youth a platform where they will learn how to fish. Today’s children are tomorrow’s future. They will reform the country and inspire the continent.



Our promise

Bolingo promises unconditional love, love for all races, religions, cultures and beliefs. We promise unity and transparency. We will stand up for what we believe in. Our motto is to "Love your neighbor as yourself". We promise to stick to our code of conduct and internal regulations.


Our approach

Access to basic education in the DRC is a big challenge, as the DRC ranks the second largest country with an estimated 3.5 million children out of school. This issue is due to immense poverty, which limits parents to afford school fees. Bolingo has 6 steps to tackle this problem:

  • 1. Locate schools both in urban and rural areas

    Bolingo’s strategy in this first step is to locate schools, which are in urgent need.

  • 2. Analyze the current situation of the school and its students

    In this step, we will use qualitative methods using questionnaires and direct interviews with school authorities, students, parents and people from the community to get firsthand informations.

  • 3. Develop projects within these schools

    After finding the issue, we will develop projects to solve the current situation.

  • 4. Get funds

    In order to finance our projects, we will raise fund and build collaboratives and strategic partnerships.

  • 5. Execute the projects

    For every project, one member will be specifically chosen to manage it.

  • 6. Monitoring and Evaluation of the projects

    After the execution of the projects, we will keep portfolios of each project's outcome. The outcome will be displayed on the webpage.
