NOTRE MÈRE DE LA PAIX

Home / NOTRE MÈRE DE LA PAIX

SCHOOL: NOTRE MÈRE DE LA PAIX. Address: QP N°198 CAC/CUBA COMMUNE DE NGALIEMA. KINSHASA (DRC) Working hours: double shift 7:30 to 12:15 / 12 to 17:00.

"The general curriculum of Congo is taught at this school. This school needs help as it lacks basic resources and materials. The learning conditions are critical, and the children do not take part in any sport or social activities."

The school is situated in Kinshasa, the capital city of the Democratic republic of Congo (DRC). The primary school Notre Mere de la paix accommodates children between the ages of 5 to 12 years old. There used to be 200 kids and 6 assigned teachers, but as a result of a combination of inadequate resources and insufficient funding only 30 kids and 2 assigned teachers remained. The school consists of 3 class rooms. The school fees are 5000 Francs congolais/ month (5$ approximately). Monthly wage of the teachers is 50$.
