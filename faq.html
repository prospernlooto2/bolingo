Frequently asked questions

  • Where will my donation go?

    Our goal is to be as transparent as possible. The money will be managed by the finance team and will be allocated to the current project’s budget.

  • How can we make sure that the projects are executed as planned?

    Our project team will choose the right member to guide, supervise and monitor the project. The project team will make sure every requirements and specifications are met. The finance team will then guarantee that the right budget is allocated to the right project, and they will monitor every expenditure.

  • Why should I donate to Bolingo?

    First of all, Bolingo represents conditional love, love with all its elements. The movement is formed by the people of The Democratic Republic of Congo, its youth, the future of the country. The team has a direct contact with the community and understands the beauty and the language of its people.

  • Why the Democratic republic of Congo?

    “Africa is shaped like a gun, and Congo is the trigger.” “Frantz Fanon’’
    The Democratic Republic of Congo is the broken heart of Africa, yet has the resources to feed the whole continent. We believe if we could achieve unity, peace and stability in this country, which is the trigger of the gun, we will make an impact in the whole African continent and the rest of the world.
