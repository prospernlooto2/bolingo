meet our members

The founders are a diverse group of youth which collectively believe in one virtue: Love. Our motto is to “love our neighbors as ourselves”. We aim to develop communities by changing lives through the way youth think. Only in union of many cultures, races, religions and beliefs that anything perfect can be formed. A single individual cannot offer you perfection, but the whole of humanity, with all its varied characteristics, may do so! We believe that every individual has something which is definitely part of the whole.
