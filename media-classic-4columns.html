About the event

Official Launch of Bolingo

Durban, South Africa, 21st May 2016

The team Bolingo had its official launch event in South Africa, Durban the 21st May 2016. The aim of the event was to share the values and objectives of Bolingo.

A well orientated Team gave a good presentation which consisted of the objectives, values, vision and missions of Bolingo. During the presentation, a brief historical background of the Democratic Republic of Congo was shared to the enthusiastic audience. The executive director of Bolingo, Mr TP Nlooto opened the presentation by sharing social and economic issues facing the vast and rich country the DRC. The presentation continued with other well presented team members of Bolingo. Miss Nokuthula 'Peace' introduced herself and stated the reason why she joined the movement,then blessed the audience with a lovely poem she composed specifically for the event. Mr David Steinebach ,the finance director of Bolingo shared with the audience the vision,mission of Bolingo and his personal reasons for joining the movement.The last presentation was done by Mr Heroique Nlooto the Secretary General also in finance who talked about the spiritual reason for forming the movement, the need to unify the youth, the promise of Bolingo and different projects the organization is working on. The entertained audience had a taste of delicious Congolese food which was offered by Bolingo's team, Prosper Nlooto and Nokuthula Magwaza hosted a competition whereby they gave away two free Bolingo t-shirts to people who won the competition that consisted of questions and answers. We then celebrated David Steinebach's birthday, followed by a music performance by Congolese artists from Durban, Christon Mukongo and fantastic guys, Pax Nabu and Dolly Matshuda. The official launch event was a success for the team as those invited shared their experiences of being there. The team in South Africa thanked everyone who came.
